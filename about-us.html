Our Story

Incorporated in 2021, in Chennai as a Private Limited Company.Started as Exclusive Indian Channel Partner for LPMS – Taiwan / USA / CHINA( Low Pressure Molding Machine Manufacturer).Started Supplying Imported Pb Free Solder paste for LED Lighting Industry.

In 2022, Partnered with Shanghai Beginor Technology, a leading manufacturer of Adhesives, Sealants , Thermal Management and Encapsulation Materials.

In 2023 Partnered with Ubond Technologies – Leading Manufacturer of Soldering materials and adhesives for Consumer Electronic Applications.Started Manufacturing RoHS Solder paste in Chennai plant with Technical assistance from U-Bond.ISO 9001:2015 Certified.Created new Joint Venture Company with FDI (Eclectic Electronic Materials) and acquired Land from SIDCO (Tamil Nadu Govt) to setup factory in Ranipet, (100km from Chennai).

In 2024 Construction of Factory in Ranipet, TN (100km from Chennai)
